# Privacy Policy for Bot de Puré

## Information We Collect
We collect and store the following user data to ensure Bot de Puré functions properly and provides a personalized experience:
- **Discord User ID** – Used to associate you with your settings and data.
- **Usage Data** – Information on command usage, economy systems, and feature interactions is stored to maintain functionality, prevent abuse, and improve analytical features.
- **Customization Settings** – Includes user preferences such as language, subscriptions (e.g., for the "PuréFeed" system), and other configurable options.
- **Confession System** – Pending confessions, including minimal data (e.g., Discord ID and confession content), are temporarily stored to facilitate confession moderation.
- **API Keys** – Stored when provided for services like Imgur, Catbox, and SauceNAO to enable certain commands.

## How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Maintain and provide Bot de Puré’s functionality, including to provide support and respond to your requests.
- Enable personalized experiences.
- Allow API key registration and third-party service interactions.
- Support moderation efforts and prevent abuse.
- We may also use your information to develop new features or services, to conduct research and analytics, and to comply with legal obligations.

## Sharing Your Information
Bot de Puré does not sell or share your personal information with third parties. However, to provide certain functionalities, it interacts with external services (e.g., Gelbooru). These services may collect data independently, and their privacy policies apply when interacting with them.

Additionally, user data may be disclosed if required by law, such as in response to legal processes or requests from law enforcement agencies or regulatory authorities.

## Data Retention
- We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide Bot de Puré’s services or as required by law.
- We will delete your information upon your request or when it is no longer needed.

## Security Measures
We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

## Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and we will post the updated policy on our website. Your continued use of Bot de Puré after we make changes to this policy indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
